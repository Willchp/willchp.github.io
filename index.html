Yo! I’m Will.
I’m a master’s student at UC Berkeley under Professor Ken Goldberg exploring the intersection of AI and robotics.

In particular, I am interested in using imitation learning and reinforcement learning to teach robots to perform precise, varied, and long-horizon tasks. My work includes explorations of multimodal generative models in robotics, visual sensing, nanorobots, and learned task and motion planning.
